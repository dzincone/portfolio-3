Lyne Up

Lyne Up was created as a basketball league scheduling application. As a League Manager of Youth Basketball League, scheduling for the season is a tedious process. Gone are the days of pen and paper, and we are now moving into the age of applications making things simple for us. Lyne up lets you create leagues. You can add locations, divisions, league settings, and teams. Coaches can sign-up and enter their team in different divisions with full team rosters. This is a simple way to create a league and fill divisions as well as keep track of all your league information right from one application.
