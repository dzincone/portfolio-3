Galvanize Ping Pong

As I entered into the programming world I quickly discovered how big Ping Pong was to the community. It represents bouncing ideas back and forth to one another, as well as being highly enjoyable. With an influx of new students and only one ping pong table at our location, we needed a way to organize games and matches while being able to keep track of who has beaten who. This application allows for users to sign up, go through the game making process, and choose opponents to play. After a match has been created, you can play your game and determine the winner. This reflects on both players profile pages with active, upcoming, and past games.
