Galvanize Ping Pong

Galvanize Ping Pong was inspired by the entire class wanting to play ping pong and not being able to keep track of matches and who is playing who. I desined this application to keep track of schedules, opponents, and who has won or lost. Resources used for this application were: Nodejs, Express, MongoDB, Monk, HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This was my first real introduction into User Authentication.
